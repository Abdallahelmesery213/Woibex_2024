Woibex – Global Women Leaders Conference Activities and 2024 Theme

Woibex, the Global Women Leaders conference held annually by Datamatix, recognizes the achievements of pioneering women leaders regionally and globally, and encourages women leaders to learn from their experiences. Providing an environment conducive to the exchange of views, discussion, healthy arguments, and face-to-face networking amongst global women leaders, the event aims to elevate the status of women in society regionally and globally, enabling them to acquire the skills needed to succeed in the new global market to face the future challenges and benefit from the opportunities.

Announcing the Middle East Top 26 Knowledge Development Women Leaders Excellence Awards

The Awards identifies a new generation of icons, game-changers, and gate-crashers who are boldly scaling new heights and transforming the world whilst paying tribute to the outstanding women leaders who have advocated for diversity in the workforce and who serve as an inspiration for the next generation of leaders.

22nd Women Leaders Excellence Awards Ceremony

The Middle East Women Leaders Excellence Awards honors the strategic and influential role of various Women leaders in the development of the modern economy, technology, and administrative methodologies that promotes the advancement of the govt. And business org. According to the standards of global competitiveness.

Global Women Pilots Delegation in Dubai

Brings together global aviation, airlines, airports, and women pilots and aviation experts from around the world to share knowledge and exchange ideas and information on the aviation industry

Global Education’s Women’s Delegation in Dubai

Brings together women leaders in education and provides valuable opportunities for networking, exchanging best practices, and discussing recent developments in higher education.

Global Women Leaders (Best Practice Showcase)

The event brings together women leaders in all fields to highlight inspiring stories of successful women leaders and showcases best practices.

Global Women-Owned Companies Gathering

Serves as a platform for women entrepreneurs and encourages them to enhance their roles as business leaders, offering networking, knowledge exchange, and advice on how to introduce and showcase their services and products, network with other businesses and build new client relations.

Global Women Authors’ and Writers’ Gathering

The event brings together leading women authors and writers to share their work, as well as collaborate, exchange knowledge, and peer-to-peer engagement whilst highlighting a range of topics related to writing, publishing, and book-selling.

Women’s Global Fashion

The event will showcase designers, stylists and fashion diversity advocates, multigenerational innovators, entrepreneurs, and influencers and aims to empower women to showcase their passion through arts. It celebrates the doers and the doing, a source of inspiration and support that connects and empowers women around the world, helping them to realize their potential and reach new heights.
