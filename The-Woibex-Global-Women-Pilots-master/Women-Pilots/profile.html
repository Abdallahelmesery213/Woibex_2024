The Woibex Global
Women Pilots Delegations to Dubai

In the visionary framework of the world’s aviation industry and leaders, and in alignment with global efforts to promote the women's empowerment and global competitiveness in aviation industry, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies and to support the “International Women’s Day.”

Datamatix, in collaboration with global governments, businesses, media, institutions, and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute, presents the 27th International Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, the congress will be held under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global government and business decision-makers, official institutions, intellectuals and women leaders. The Woibex Congress offers a unique opportunity for women to learn about best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

In this context, Datamatix with the support of the leading aviation organizations, and Airlines presents this special highlight of the congress, The Woibex Global Women Pilots Delegation to Dubai.

This event will unite women leaders from the global aviation and airlines industry, inspiring new initiatives for growth and innovation. It will showcase best practices, strategies, and visionary approaches aimed at developing programs that support and empower women pilots, facilitating them to overcome challenges and highlighting their successes. Women pilots in commercial, military, and private aviation, along with airline leaders and other aviation organizations, will address critical issues like aviation safety, technological advancements, and sustainability. This gathering creates a collaborative platform for strategic discussions and the development of pioneering solutions, fostering a more inclusive and dynamic future for the aviation industry.

In this context, we request your organization to form a delegation to join this esteemed Congress, the event highlights the special celebration designed to admire Global Women Pilots and promote the accomplishments of women in aviation across all seven continents, recognizing their invaluable contributions to society, business, and governance. Women pilots at all stages of their careers will benefit from senior pilots' mentorship, offering guidance, support, and encouragement to aspiring and early-career pilots to take on leadership roles in the aviation industry.

Therefore, we encourage all women pilot to join the congress and demonstrate their commitment to advancing women's leadership in aviation. By participating, you will have the opportunity to highlight your institution's innovative programs, share best practices, and collaborate with other global aviation leaders. This event provides a unique platform to showcase your dedication to empowering women in the aviation industry and contribute to the development of a more inclusive and forward-thinking aviation community.

The Woibex Global Women Pilot Excellence Award

Datamatix with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and the publishers and authors, and in collaboration with global government organizations, businesses corporates, media institutions and with the support of the official global institutions presents, “Global Women Pilot Excellence Award,” during the 27th International Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE.

The Award will showcase the remarkable achievements of women who have demonstrated exceptional leadership and their outstanding contributions to advancing flight safety, aviation technology, and the overall development of the industry, including improvements in aircraft design, advancements in avionics, enhancements in air traffic management, innovations in pilot training programs, and contributions to sustainable aviation practices. The award celebrates the dedication of women pilot’s excellence who have excelled in their professional roles and served as inspiring mentors and role models for future generations of aviation women leaders. The award also emphasizes the importance of diversity and inclusion within the aviation industry, by highlighting the successes of women pilots from around all the seven continents, encouraging more women to enter and thrive in this traditionally male-dominated field. The stories of these aviators serve as powerful examples of accomplishment, outstanding skill, determination, and opportunity converge.

In this regard, Woibex deeply values your support in advancing women in the aviation sector, as it plays a crucial role in empowering women, enhancing global women competitiveness, fostering women leadership, and promoting excellence. Therefore, we encourage you to join the event and nominate yourself or other outstanding women in aviation sector for consideration of this prestigious award. The award aims to honor women in the aviation industry who have made profound contributions to shaping aviation policies and advancing aeronautical innovation within their institutions and beyond for excellence in aviation. Based on the Institute's procedure, nominations will be accepted until February 10th, 2025.

The Awards Subcategories

  • Lifetime Achievement in Aviation
  • Outstanding Women Commercial Pilot
  • Excellence in Military Aviation
  • Innovative Women Flight Instructor
  • Pioneering Aviation Women Engineer
  • Distinguished Air Traffic Women Controller

  • Exceptional Aerobatic Women Pilot
  • Outstanding Contribution to Aviation Technology
