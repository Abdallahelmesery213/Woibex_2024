Subject: Knowledge Influencer Partner
Supporting the (Women Leaders' Influencer Delegation to Dubai)

In the visionary framework of the world’s leaders, and in alignment with global efforts to promote the women's empowerment, gender balance, leadership, and women’s global competitiveness, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies and support the “International Women’s Day.”

In this context, Datamatix, in collaboration with global governments, businesses, media and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute, presents the 27th Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, the congress will be held under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global government and business decision-makers, official institutions, intellectuals and women leaders, offers a unique opportunity for women to learn about best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together women leaders and influencers, to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

As a successful knowledge influencer, we are honored to communicate with you to join the 27th Women Leaders Congress as a Woibex 2025 Knowledge Influencer Partner, promoting the women empowerment through knowledge exchange with your followers globally, also in this context, we request you to form a delegation under your name and invite all women leaders from your followers, to join the delegation to attend the Congress.

By encouraging women worldwide to attend the congress, your participation will play a pivotal role in shaping the narrative around women's leadership and empowerment. This collaboration will significantly amplify your influence, positioning you as a global leadership advocate for women's empowerment and advocate for transformative change.

Your support in encouraging the participation of female leaders from all global organizations and forming a delegation to attend the congress will have a profound impact. Leading a delegation enhances your social profile and establishes you as a global advocate for positive change. To lead a delegation, we suggest utilizing your digital presence to promote the congress and encourage women to register under your delegation’s name and join the congress.

Also, as recognition, the influencers who attract the highest number of registered women leaders in their delegation will be honored by the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute with one of the Woibex 7Continents Influencers Excellence Awards, for their exceptional efforts in advancing women’s empowerment. This prestigious recognition will solidify your status as a leader in the movement for women's empowerment. Additionally, Datamatix offers significant rewards, including elevated social profile visibility, unique marketing opportunities, and more based on the rewards mentioned in this proposal.

Once again, we would like to welcome you as a Woibex 2025 Knowledge Influencer Partner, and an opportunity for you to leverage your social media platforms to spread the news about this impactful event, encouraging women worldwide to join the congress via your delegation and platform.

Yours Sincerely,
