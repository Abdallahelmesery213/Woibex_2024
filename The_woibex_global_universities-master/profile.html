The Woibex Global Universities Women’s Leaders Delegation in Dubai

In the visionary framework of the world’s educationalist leaders, and in alignment with global efforts to promote the women's empowerment, gender balance, leadership, and women’s competitiveness, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies and to support the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

Datamatix, in collaboration with global governments, universities, businesses, institutions, and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute, presents the 27th international Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global universities and educational institutions decision-makers, intellectuals and women leaders. The Congress offers a unique opportunity about best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

Datamatix, with the support of the international educational and the academic organizations, presents this special highlight of the congress, The Woibex Global Universities Women’s Leaders Delegation in Dubai. The gathering of women educational leaders will foster a deeper appreciation for women's contributions to the world education and encourage the development of new educational initiatives that promote the world diversity and leadership within academic institutions.

In this context, we request your university to form a delegation and encouraging all women in the university to attend the Congress. Also on this occasion, we would like to suggest that the university to invite and communicate officially with the top Women Leaders to join the delegation, and could be women leaders from different organizations, and comprising representatives from the official institutions, Ministries, Government Organizations, States and Municipalities, Tourism Services Providers, Artists, Businesswomen, Chamber of Commences, Universities, Women Associations and Whoever is interested to participate in the 27th Women Leaders Congress. This esteemed event is designed to promote the accomplishments of women across all seven continents, recognizing their invaluable contributions to society, business, and governance.

The Congress will honor the efforts of universities in advancing innovative programs and initiatives that support and empower women at all levels of academia and beyond, the Congress aims to inspire universities to adopt best practices, fostering a global movement towards more inclusive and equitable educational environments. This dynamic interaction seeks to elevate the standards of higher education globally, ensuring that all women have the resources and support needed to succeed and lead.

Therefore, we encourage your Universities Women’s Leaders to join the congress and showcase your commitment to advancing women's leadership in education by participating, you will have the opportunity to highlight your institution's innovative programs, share best practices, and collaborate with other global leaders to drive meaningful change and create more inclusive and equitable educational environments.

Women Leaders' Delegation to Dubai Overview and Profile.

The Congress is a prestigious event that brings together influential women leaders from universities around the world. Set in the vibrant city of Dubai, renowned for its innovation and cosmopolitan culture, this delegation aims to foster global collaboration, knowledge exchange, and leadership development among women in higher education. Participants will have the unique opportunity to engage with peers, share best practices, and discuss the latest trends and challenges in academia. The event will feature keynote speeches, panel discussions, and interactive workshops led by prominent female leaders in education, policy, and industry. These sessions are designed to inspire and empower women leaders to drive positive change within their institutions and beyond.

The delegation will also be provided with a platform for networking, enabling participants to build lasting connections with global counterparts. By attending, delegates will contribute to advancing gender equality and leadership diversity in higher education, enhancing their own professional growth and institutional impact.

On this occasion, we respectfully request your Excellency’s approval to form a Women Leaders Delegation comprising representatives from the ministry, schools, universities, and other educational organizations. This delegation will act as ambassadors for your country’s educational sector, significantly impacting and enhancing future endeavors. Their participation will support your government's values and objectives, facilitate valuable connections, and promote sustainable education, cultural exchange, knowledge sharing, and bilateral business relations.

Participation in the 27th International Women Leaders Congress offers the Global Universities Women’s Leaders Delegation a unique and invaluable opportunity to engage with a global community of women leaders across various sectors. This Congress serves as a pivotal platform for exchanging insights, fostering collaborations, and advocating for women’s empowerment and leadership development on a global scale. Attending the Congress provides delegates with access to a diverse range of perspectives and experiences, enriching their understanding of the challenges and opportunities for delegates to delve into innovative strategies, best practices, and transformative initiatives that promote continuous improvement and excellence in educational technologies, and enhancing teaching methodologies, fostering inclusive learning environments online and on campus, promoting student engagement, addressing the evolving needs of diverse student populations and promoting the excellence in educational sector This collaborative exchange of insights fosters a culture of innovation and empowers women delegates to implement impactful changes within their institutions and communities, ensuring that education remains a cornerstone of societal progress.

The Woibex Global Women Leaders in Education Excellence Award

Datamatix, with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and global educational institutions, alongside renowned global universities, colleagues and research centers, and in collaboration with global government organizations, business corporates, media institutions, and with the support of official global institutions, takes the honor of presenting the “Global Women Leader in Education Excellence Award” in conjunction with the 27th International Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE.

The Award will showcase the remarkable achievements of women who have demonstrated exceptional leadership and innovation in education. These women have significantly contributed to advancing educational practices, creating inclusive and equitable learning environments, developing innovative academic programs, and enhancing student outcomes. The award celebrates the dedication and hard work of women who excel in their professional roles and serve as inspiring mentors for future educators and leaders. This prestigious award acknowledges the profound impact women leaders have on the academic landscape, shaping educational policies, advocating meaningful reforms, and implementing strategies that drive institutional success. Their commitment to fostering environments where all students can thrive and addressing critical issues like access to quality education and equity paves the way for a brighter, more inclusive future in education.

In this regard, Woibex deeply values your support in advancing women in the educational sector, as it plays a crucial role in empowering women, enhancing global women competitiveness, fostering women leadership, and promoting excellence. Therefore, we encourage you to join the event and nominate yourself or other deserving women in educational sector for consideration of this prestigious award. Based on the Institute's procedure, nominations will be accepted until February 10th, 2025.

The Award’s subcategories

  • Academia Leadership Excellence Award
  • Innovation in Education Award
  • Advancement of Student Success Award
  • Community Engagement and Outreach Educational Excellence Award
  • Research Excellence Award
  • Global Education Impact Award
  • Educational Lifetime Achievement Award
  • Rising Star in Education Excellence Award
  • Sustainability and Environmental Education Award
  • Inspirational Mentorship Award
  • Modern management in Educational Excellence Award
