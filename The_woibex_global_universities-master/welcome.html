Subject: The Woibex
Global Universities Women’s Leaders Delegation in Dubai

In the visionary framework of the world’s educationalist leaders, and in alignment with global efforts to promote the women's empowerment, gender balance, leadership, and women’s competitiveness, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies and to support the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

Datamatix, in collaboration with global governments, universities, businesses, institutions, and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute, presents the 27th international Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global universities and educational institutions decision-makers, intellectuals and women leaders. The Congress offers a unique opportunity about best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

Datamatix, with the support of the international educational and the academic organizations, presents this special highlight of the congress, The Woibex Global Universities Women’s Leaders Delegation in Dubai. The gathering of women educational leaders will foster a deeper appreciation for women's contributions to the world education and encourage the development of new educational initiatives that promote the world diversity and leadership within academic institutions.

In this context, we request your university to form a delegation and encouraging all women in the university to attend the Congress. Also on this occasion, we would like to suggest that the university to invite and communicate officially with the top Women Leaders to join the delegation, and could be women leaders from different organizations, and comprising representatives from the official institutions, Ministries, Government Organizations, States and Municipalities, Tourism Services Providers, Artists, Businesswomen, Chamber of Commences, Universities, Women Associations and Whoever is interested to participate in the 27th Women Leaders Congress. This esteemed event is designed to promote the accomplishments of women across all seven continents, recognizing their invaluable contributions to society, business, and governance.

The Congress will honor the efforts of universities in advancing innovative programs and initiatives that support and empower women at all levels of academia and beyond, the Congress aims to inspire universities to adopt best practices, fostering a global movement towards more inclusive and equitable educational environments. This dynamic interaction seeks to elevate the standards of higher education globally, ensuring that all women have the resources and support needed to succeed and lead.

Therefore, we encourage your Universities Women’s Leaders to join the congress and showcase your commitment to advancing women's leadership in education by participating, you will have the opportunity to highlight your institution's innovative programs, share best practices, and collaborate with other global leaders to drive meaningful change and create more inclusive and equitable educational environments.
