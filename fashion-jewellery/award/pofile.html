The Woibex Global Women Fashion and Jewelry Award

The Woibex Global Women Fashion and Jewelry Awards is a prestigious event that celebrates and empowers women leaders in the fashion and jewelry industries, held in the glamorous city of Dubai. This award serves as an inspiration for innovation, creativity, and excellence, highlighting the remarkable achievements of women who have reshaped the landscape of fashion and jewelry. These industries, influenced by trendsetting designers, celebrities, and influencers, converge to honor outstanding women who deserve recognition for their vision, craftsmanship, entrepreneurship, and influence in shaping global fashion trends and jewelry design.

In this context, Datamatix, with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and global fashion and jewelry associations, in collaboration with global government organizations, business corporates, and media institutions, and with the support of official global institutions, takes the honor of presenting the “Global Women Fashion and Jewelry Excellence Award,” in conjunction with the 27th International Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 February 2025 in Dubai, UAE.

The Woibex Global Women Fashion and Jewelry Award will celebrate the achievements of women who have demonstrated exceptional creativity, innovation, and leadership in the fashion and jewelry industries. It aims to recognize their contributions to setting trends, advancing craftsmanship, and reshaping the global landscape of fashion and jewelry design. This prestigious award highlights the entrepreneurial spirit of women who have built successful brands, influenced consumer tastes, and fostered industry advancements. By honoring these women, the award not only celebrates their individual successes but also promotes gender diversity and empowerment within the fashion and jewelry sectors, inspiring others to pursue excellence in these fields.

In this regard, Woibex deeply values your support in advancing women in the fashion and jewelry industry and promoting excellence. Therefore, we encourage you to join the event and nominate yourself or other outstanding women in the fashion and jewelry sector for consideration of this prestigious award. Your participation will contribute to recognizing and celebrating women who have made remarkable contributions to these industries. Based on the Institute's procedure, nominations will be accepted until February 10th, 2025, providing an opportunity to showcase outstanding achievements and leadership in fashion and jewelry design.

Women Fashion and Jewelry Excellence Award

The Award will showcase the remarkable achievements of women who have demonstrated exceptional talent, creativity, and leadership in the fashion and jewelry industries. These women have significantly influenced the industry through their innovative designs, unmatchable craftsmanship, and visionary approaches to style and elegance. The award celebrates their accomplishments, inspiring the next generation of female designers, artisans, and entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams. This prestigious award honors women who have excelled across diverse facets of the fashion and jewelry sectors, recognizing pioneers who set new standards of excellence. It also celebrates strides in sustainable fashion and ethical jewelry practices, promoting a more responsible industry.

In this regard, Woibex deeply values your support in advancing women in fashion and jewelry industry and promoting excellence. Therefore, we encourage you to join the event and nominate yourself or other outstanding women in fashion and jewelry sector for consideration of this prestigious award. Based on the Institute's procedure, nominations will be accepted until February 10th, 2025.
