The Woibex Global Businesswomen Excellence Award

Global businesswomen are powerful drivers of economic growth and innovation, their leadership and entrepreneurial spirit have led to significant advancements in various industries. These women break barriers and redefine success, fostering inclusive workplaces and sustainable business practices, extending beyond the corporate world, as they advocate social causes and community development. By mentoring the next generation and advocating for gender equality, global businesswomen inspire others to pursue their ambitions. Their contributions are crucial in shaping a more equitable and prosperous global economy, showcasing the transformative power of diversity and leadership.

In this context, Datamatix, with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and global business organizations, media institutions, along with the support from official global institutions, presents the "The Woibex Global Businesswomen Excellence Award" during the 27th International Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 February 2025 in Dubai, UAE. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

The award demonstrates the global collective commitment to businesswomen's development, advancing global unity via business, and supporting international collaboration through business connections. This esteemed award features a special recognition for businesswomen, designed to honor their achievements and dedication to economic growth The Woibex Global Businesswomen award is strategically held in Dubai, renowned for its pioneering business environment and cosmopolitan culture. It honors outstanding female entrepreneurs, executives, and industry leaders from across the globe, aiming to empower women in business by providing a unique platform for showcasing their success.

In this context, we request your organization to demonstrate your commitment to advancing women's leadership in business by nominating yourself or other outstanding businesswomen for consideration of this prestigious award. Present your best female leaders by showcasing your achievements, innovations, leadership qualities, and community contributions. Based on the Institute's procedure, nominations will be accepted until February 10th, 2025.
