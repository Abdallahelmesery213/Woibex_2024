The Woibex Global Businesswomen Delegation to Dubai

In the visionary framework of the world’s business institutions and leaders, and in alignment with global economic efforts to promote the Businesswomen empowerment and women’s global competitiveness, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies.

Datamatix in association with the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and with the support of the governments, businesses and media, presents the 27th Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, the congress will be held under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global decision-makers, intellectuals and women leaders, it offers a unique opportunity for women to learn about the best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

In this context, Datamatix, with the support of the global business and investment institutions, with great pleasure presents this special highlight of the congress, The Woibex Global Businesswomen Delegation to Dubai.

The event demonstrates the global collective commitment to Businesswomen development, advancing global unity via Business, and supporting cultural harmony through Business connections. This esteemed congress features a special global networking event for businesswomen, designed to bring together the Global Businesswomen Delegations. It connects outstanding female entrepreneurs, executives, and industry leaders from across the globe. Its aim is to empower women in business by providing a unique platform for business networking, knowledge exchange, and collaboration. The Woibex Global Businesswomen Delegation to Dubai is strategically held in a Dubai City, renowned for its pioneering business environment and cosmopolitan culture. The event highlights the pivotal role of women in driving economic growth and innovation in today’s global marketplace.

In this context, we request your organization to form a businesswomen delegation to join the Congress by encouraging women leaders to attend and demonstrate your commitment to advancing women's leadership in business. By participating, the businesswomen will have the opportunity to highlight their corporation’s innovative programs, share best practices, and collaborate with other global women business leaders.

The Congress provides a unique platform to showcase your dedication to empowering women in the business industry and to contribute to the development of a more inclusive and forward-thinking women's business community.

The Woibex Global Businesswomen Excellence

The Woibex Global Businesswomen Excellence Award is a prestigious honor celebrating the remarkable achievements of women in the business world. This award recognizes female leaders who have demonstrated exceptional talent, innovation, and leadership in their respective fields, setting new standards of excellence and driving significant advancements within their industries. By acknowledging these outstanding women, the award aims to inspire the next generation of female entrepreneurs and business leaders to pursue their ambitions with confidence and determination. The award showcases the diverse contributions of women across various sectors, including finance, technology, healthcare, education, manufacturing, and retail. These awardees have not only excelled in their professional endeavors but have also made significant impacts through their commitment to social responsibility, ethical business practices, and community engagement. Their stories of resilience, innovation, and strategic thinking serve as powerful examples of what can be achieved when talent, hard work, and opportunity intersect.
