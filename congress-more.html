International Women Leaders Congress

Year of the Women Role Models

We sincerely appreciate your organization's constructive efforts in supporting women role models and recognizing their influence on young women leaders worldwide. Your dedication to advancing women is evident, as it contributes to the country's global competitiveness, fosters leadership, promotes excellence, and empowers emerging young women leaders to become the next generation of Women Role Models.

After, a successful Woibex Silver Jubilee Celebration, where astonishing global women leaders gathered to cherish their best practices and achievements, in the presence of global leaders and the prominent figure Ms. Maye Musk, where the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute has honored her with the prestigious awards “Women of the Year and the Lifetime Achievement Award-2023” with the participation of the global women leaders and the media.

In this context, Datamatix in association with its strategic global government, business, and official institutions partners and in presence of the global leaders, and in conjunction with the International Women’s Day celebration, with great pleasure presents the 26th International Women Leaders Congress on 6-8 March 2024 in Dubai-UAE,
attended by international women role model, women leaders and senior officials from the official global institutions and the public and private sector organisations, media, and women's business organizations.

The congress aims to explore modern international leadership strategies to nurture the evolution of women leaders into role models, fostering the growth and aspiring the next generation of women role models, and providing support for young women leaders, in shaping international initiatives furthermore, the congress seeks to fortify the competitiveness of the women role models in the forthcoming era of global changes and will also address the directions and aspirations of contemporary role models, illuminating vital pathways for their growth while keeping pace with global progress and future modern management strategies.

In this regard, with great pleasure we communicate with your esteemed organizations for the women leader participation in this international event, as their presence will greatly contribute to the success of their future and will foster the global discussion on how to transform the young leaders.

The Congress is a global platform to exchange experiences and the best practices in empowering the next generation of Women Role Models and networking and to exchange international experiences.

Global Vision of the 26th International Women Leaders Congress
(Year of the Women Role Models)

Woibex, the 26th International women Leaders Congress is one of the most significant international platforms for exchanging experiences and showcasing the achievements of women leaders who have contributed to the development and implementation of best international leadership and institutional practices. It focuses on empowering and nurturing women leaders in modern management science, corporate and leadership governance, knowledge management, and innovation strategies based on global competitiveness standards.

The International women Leaders Congress has achieved both regional and international praise and it is emerging as the largest event showcasing women leaders in the international forums. Over the past quarter of a century, this event has played a vital role in bolstering the foundations of global competitiveness for leadership excellence and enhancing the management of administrative leadership systems to promote the growth of role models and empower women leaders in the Middle East. Through its efforts, Congress has successfully attracted best practices and global competitiveness standards to the Middle East.

In continuation of the vision of the International women Leaders Congress, Datamatix, in cooperation with its international partners from the public and private sectors and with the support of the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and various media, is honored to announce the Global Vision of Woibex – 26th International Women Leaders Congress 2024, titled: "Year of Women Leaders as Role Models." The congress aims to identify leadership models and empower them to transition into role models and frontline leaders in government and private sector institutions, as well as in the media, and international organizations.

Role model leaders are those who have made significant contributions to leading organizations worldwide, particularly in the context of the modern digital economy. These leaders serve as exceptional examples, showcasing successful executive figures who play a pivotal role in the development of the leadership system and enable their

teams to embrace foresight and shape the future through the latest modern management science systems, institutional creativity, and global competitiveness standards. Their expertise extends to developing sustainability management and governance within organizations, inspiring the next generation of women leaders to take prominent positions in both public and private sector institutions.

These role models are essential in driving positive changes and achieving organizational success. These successful leaders have demonstrated exceptional leadership skills and governance management, adhering to the latest standards of modern management, institutional innovation, and global competitiveness.

The International women Leaders Congress 2024 aims to highlight such role models who have made significant contributions and served as exemplary leaders. Their successes will be presented to enable other women leaders to learn from their best practices and experiences. The Congress also aims to motivate young women leaders to ascend the ranks of distinguished leadership and emerge as role models themselves within the next 5 years.

The Middle East Excellence Awards Institute

Coinciding with the Middle East Excellence Award Institute's vision for 2030, which focuses on developing and sustaining global institutional and leadership competitiveness in the region, this event is based on the Institute's role in showcasing the latest practices and achievements in institutional leadership. It also highlights sustainable excellence strategies that align with the region’s goal of transforming into an integrated digital economy, embracing Fifth-G technologies and the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

In this context, the Institute has been actively supporting and honoring leadership capabilities, institutional successes, excellence, and sustainable leadership achievements in both the public and private sectors for the past 34 years. This support is carried out through regional and global research, as well as field studies in the selection management of individuals who have contributed to the development process and building the future of the region. These efforts have resulted in consolidating the principles of leadership in various sectors, creating a base that plays an effective role in supporting development initiatives.

34 years ago, the Institute embarked on its development journey to contribute to the advancement of excellence and leadership governance while celebrating institutional and leadership achievements. The goal was to accelerate institutional and organizational development and sustainable leadership in the Middle East through the Institute's Excellence Awards events and programs.

Additionally, the Institute places significant importance on honoring best practices and competencies in various areas such as leadership, social, economic, media, technology, knowledge management, innovation, and women's leadership. It also highlights the significance of organizational integration and digital transformation efforts to advance government and digital cities while enhancing the competitiveness of digital cities in the era of 5G, artificial intelligence, blockchain technologies, and the Internet of Things.

Over the past four decades, the Institute has been developing leadership and comprehensive institutional growth, elevating the region's administrative efficiency to global competitiveness standards. It has supported leaders and institutions in making decisions based on modern management science, enhancing knowledge, and improving overall efficiency and effectiveness. These strategies and events have successfully bridged the digital, economic, and knowledge gap between the Middle East and the rest of the world, playing a vital role in fostering excellence.

The participation of leaders and decision-makers in these events has significantly contributed to global partnerships, solidified international relations between institutions, decision-makers, and media organizations, and facilitated the exchange of experiences, bringing world civilizations and cultures closer together. The Institute's programs and awards are regarded as essential global events for government and economic sector leaders and institutions. These awards have aided in developing competitive strategies and administrative governance, supporting development strategies, and leveraging standards to bridge the digital, economic, and knowledge gap in the region.

The Institute has consistently aimed to explore modern international leadership strategies, nurturing the evolution of leaders into role models, fostering growth for the next generation, and supporting emerging leaders. Additionally, the Institute seeks to sustain competitiveness in the forthcoming era of global changes. It addresses contemporary leaders' directions and aspirations by honoring and recognizing them with awards, illuminating vital pathways for growth while aligning with future modern management strategies.
