Greetings,

In the context to achieving global gender balance and fostering an environment that provides the opportunity to contribute and partner in building the world's future. Datamatix takes the honor of announcing, the Woibex 2024, which aims to promote global knowledge sharing and development.

On this global occasion, we take the honor of requesting your organization's support and partnership for the ‘26th Global Women Leaders Conference, which will be held in Dubai, UAE, from the 6th to 8th of March 2024.

As a courtesy from your Excellency, we formally request your organization, to organize a Corporate Women Leaders Delegation to Dubai and sponsor the trip of the Hard-Working women leaders and executives from your organization and distinguished women Leaders from your Valuable Customers globally. By sponsoring the delegation trip, they are able to be your Corporate Women Ambassadors whilst endorsing your organization's values and augmenting the objective of establishing a fruitful relationship with the intellectually women leaders.

The event aims to highlight global women’s achievements and gathers women leaders from different countries, states, provinces, cities, and regions, which includes government, business, media, women's associations, global official institutions , and NGOs under one roof ,thereby facilitating unprecedented access to distinguished women leaders while providing business exposure for your corporate and brand and gain from the direct contact with potential clients regionally and globally.

Your support will enable us to promote the exchange of knowledge in various sectors to support the opening of new horizons for women leaders and foster an environment that provides an opportunity to contribute and partner in building the future.

The event is an important opportunity for global women leaders to promote the exchange of best practices, knowledge on leadership, women's empowerment ,and the world civilizations and culture to have a world with peace and leadership.

We hope to have your personal support in this mission.

Ali Al Kamali
Managing Director, Datamatix
kamali@datamatixgroup.com
