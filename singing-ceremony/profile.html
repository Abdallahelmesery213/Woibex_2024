The Woibex 25 Global Female Authors
Book Showcasing and Signing Ceremony

In the visionary framework of the world’s leaders, and in alignment with global knowledge development efforts to promote the women's knowledge empowerment, and global competitiveness, there is a collective exploration of international leadership strategies and to support the “International Women’s Day.”

Datamatix in association with the Middle East Excellence Awards Institute and with the support of the governments, businesses and media, presents the 27th Women Leaders Congress, on 17-20 Feb 2025 in Dubai, UAE, the congress will be held under the theme “The 7Continents Success Stories of Women” attended by global decision-makers, intellectuals and women leaders, it offers a unique opportunity for women to learn about the best practices, advance modern strategies, and discuss challenges. It brings together global women leaders, influencers, and visionaries to shape the future of women's empowerment and leadership.

In this context, Datamatix with the support of the publishers and authors presents this special highlight of the congress, The Woibex 25 Global Female Authors Book Showcasing and Signing Ceremony.

The event will celebrate the literary contributions of remarkable women authors, whose works have made significant impacts in various fields. The book signing ceremony will provide an exclusive platform for these authors to present their work, share their insights, and connect with an international audience. Authors participating in the Book Showcasing and Signing ceremony represent a wide array of genres including fiction, non-fiction, poetry, memoir, and academic writing. Each author has demonstrated exceptional creativity, skill, and dedication to their craft, inspiring readers worldwide with their works. It aims to amplify the voices of women authors who have challenged norms, shared unique perspectives, and enriched cultural understanding through their writings.

Additionally, this celebration of literary excellence will foster a deeper appreciation for women's contributions to literature and encourage ongoing dialogue about the power of words in driving social change.

Therefore, we encourage you to join the event and showcase your masterpiece of writing to world leaders and influential figures. Your participation will not only elevate your profile as an author but also contribute to the broader movement of empowering women in literature. The Woibex 25 Global Female Authors Book Showcasing and Signing Ceremony offers a unique platform to celebrate your achievements, connect with a global audience, and inspire future generations of women writers.

The Woibex Global Women Authors Excellence Award

Date: 5pm-7pm Thursday 20 Feb 2025 Dubai, UAE

The Award celebrates the literary contributions of remarkable women authors whose works have significantly impacted various fields. It recognizes the outstanding achievements of female writers who have enriched literature with their creativity, innovation, and leadership. Spanning multiple genres, including fiction, non-fiction, poetry, memoirs, and academic writing, these authors demonstrate exceptional skill and dedication to their craft.

The award amplifies the voices of women authors who have excelled in their literary endeavors and inspired readers worldwide. By honoring these writers, the award promotes cultural awareness and appreciation for women's contributions to literature. Women authors from diverse backgrounds and fields are encouraged to nominate themselves if they have demonstrated exceptional leadership, creativity, and impact through their writing. These nominations highlight the diversity of talent among women globally at Woibex. Nominees are selected from various genres and disciplines, serving as inspirational figures to aspiring writers and readers globally. Their works inspire readers to embrace diversity, pursue passions fearlessly, advance gender equality, and empower women authors worldwide. Woibex values the Global Women Authors' support in elevating the status of women leaders, greatly contributing to women's empowerment, global competitiveness, leadership, and excellence.
